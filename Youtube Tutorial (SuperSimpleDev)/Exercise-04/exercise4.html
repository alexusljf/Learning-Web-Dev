Notes
Margin is space outside of element
Padding is space inside of element, buttons have default of 6 on sides, 1 on top and bottom
Long text might overflow out of the border, use padding instead of height and width to solve this
By default, borders take 1px, so reduce padding-top and padding-bottom by 1px to equal the heights
