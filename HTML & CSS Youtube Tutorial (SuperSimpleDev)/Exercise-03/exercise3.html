Hovers, Transition, Shadows

Notes
:hover is a pseudo-class, adds extra styles in a certain situation
:active is when we click on the element
Transition takes 2 properties: what we want to transition, and how long we want it to take
Box-shadow takes 4 values: horizontal(to the right), vertical(downwards), blur and the colour
rgba has a 4th value, the opacity
Border-radius around half of smaller of height and width to get really rounded corners
